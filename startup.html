Startup Incubators

  • Y Combinator

    Y Combinator is one of the most prestigious startup incubators globally. It provides funding, mentorship, and a three-month program for early-stage startups.

  • Techstars

    Techstars operates a network of startup accelerators across various cities and industries. They offer mentorship, funding, and a three-month intensive program.

  • 500 Startups

    500 Startups is a global venture capital firm and seed accelerator. They invest in early-stage startups and provide access to mentors, office space, and resources.

  • StartX

    StartX is a nonprofit startup accelerator affiliated with Stanford University. It supports Stanford-affiliated entrepreneurs by providing resources, mentorship, and a vibrant community.

  • Founders Factory

    Founders Factory is a corporate-backed startup accelerator and incubator. They provide support in various sectors, including AI, health tech, and fintech.

  • MassChallenge

    MassChallenge is a global network of startup accelerators. They offer a four-month program, mentorship, and access to a vast network of experts and resources.

  • Plug and Play

    Plug and Play is a global innovation platform and accelerator that connects startups with corporations and investors. They operate across various industries.
